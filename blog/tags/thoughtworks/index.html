Since the launch of Cloud Carbon Footprint (CCF), our team always aimed to maintain and develop flexibility in both how the tool is used and the options for its deployment. After all, it is a great way to get your feet wet with CCF without bringing extra dependencies on your local machine, tying up any resources, and enabling options for an always-on service that expands beyond your local machine.
